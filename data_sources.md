# Data Sources — POPs Beyond PFAS Monte Carlo Simulation

Channel parameters in `mc_simulation.py` trace to the sources listed here.
The paper (POPs Beyond PFAS: Bioaccumulation Ratchet) is available on SSRN and contains the full
reference list and §4 calibration narrative.

---

## Channel Sources

### `C1_health_bioaccumulation`

Bioaccumulation, cancer, endocrine disruption. Sources: Stockholm Convention, WHO, IARC.

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*

### `C2_ecosystem_contamination`

Global water/soil/biota contamination, Arctic transport. Sources: AMAP, UNEP.

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*

### `C3_remediation_costs`

Superfund-scale cleanup, perpetual monitoring. Sources: EPA, Stockholm COP.

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*

### `C4_intergenerational_exposure`

Prenatal exposure, breast milk, epigenetics. Sources: Lancet, EHP.

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*

### `C5_regulatory_lag`

Stockholm listing delays 10+ yrs, exemption abuse. Sources: UNEP, IPEN.

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*
